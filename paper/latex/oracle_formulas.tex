\section{Oracle Shapley Formulations}
The marginal contribution of component $i$ is calculated as:
\begin{equation}
\phi_i(v) = \sum_{S \subseteq N \setminus \{i\}} \frac{|S|! (n - |S| - 1)!}{n!} (v(S \cup \{i\}) - v(S))
\end{equation}
